AI-powered healthcare diagnostics


AI in healthcare is a growing initiative and is improving drastically throughout the years. Generally, healthcare is expensive due to many factors including long hours and years of medical training. With AI powered healthcare diagnostics, you can get early results for your health to prevent further progression of any disease. This cuts cost of doctor visits and examination fees. If diseases and poor health is caught early, it is generally easier and less expensive to treat. It would be useful to me by monitoring my health to let me kow when I need to see a doctor vs when it's something minor that will go away soon.


Ethically, this idea is risky. Information is power and these devices and technologies collect sensitive information from it's users. These AI technologies may also develop bias and descrimination leading to unequal treatment of individuals. When relying on AI automation, healthcare providers and patients must understand how the system makes decisions and how much and how accurate the data it is actually trained on is. This helps to be cautious and helps you know how much trust to put into the system.


To prevent access to sensitive personal data, I would suggest encryption, data anonymisation or hashing. Periodically updating the system is also recommended to ensure accurate information free from bias and descrimination.
